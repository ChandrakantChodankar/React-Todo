## ğŸ¯ Usage

#### 1. Adding a Todo

- Type your task in the input field (minimum 4 characters)
- Click the "Add" button or press Enter
- Your todo appears in the list below

#### 2. Completing a Todo

- Click the green checkmark button to mark as complete
- Click again to mark as incomplete
- Completed todos show with strike-through text

#### 3. Editing a Todo

- Click the blue edit button
- The todo text appears in the input field
- Modify and click Add to save changes

#### 4. Deleting a Todo

- Click the red trash button to remove a single todo
- Click "Clear All" to remove all todos at once
